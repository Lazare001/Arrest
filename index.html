NotLazare1’s Prank Gone Wrong: The Mystery of Joe the Strider

By Staff Reporter — Pibble SMP Bureau

What began as a harmless one-hour prank has turned into one of the most bizarre tragedies ever recorded on the Pibble SMP server.

Player NotLazare1 confessed to planning a small scare for Ulane_, the proud owner of a beloved female Strider named Joe — a calm, lava-loving creature adored for her courage and red glow.

“It was supposed to be funny,” NotLazare1 told The Pibble SMP Gazette. “I wanted her to think Joe had died — just for a minute.”

To stage the prank, NotLazare1 discovered a wolf, named it Joe, and secretly relocated the real Strider to a different area, ensuring she would be safe. The plan? Make the chat display a fake death message.

The only other player online, Abdul, was called in for help — but had no idea how to make that happen.

“We tried everything,” said NotLazare1. “Then I just… used lava.”

Moments later, the fateful message appeared: “Joe died.”

Ulane_ was horrified. She sprinted to Joe’s pen — only to find it empty. When confronted, NotLazare1 admitted it was all a joke, assuring her that Joe was alive and tethered elsewhere.

Together, they rushed to the location — but it was too late. Joe was gone.

Server logs showed no official cause of death, yet evidence suggested a grim truth: the rain had begun moments earlier. Since Joe was roped to a fence, unable to reach lava for warmth, her fiery spirit had simply… faded away.

⚖️ Verdict:

Crime: Reckless pranking and accidental Strider manslaughter.

Sentence: Lifetime banishment from every city: “creative jokes involving lava.”
